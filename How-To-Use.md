# Budget Tracker — Quick Start

Open **index.html** in any browser (double-click it). On a phone, use your browser's
**Add to Home Screen** so it opens like an app. Tap the ☾ in the top corner for dark mode.

## First-time setup (3 minutes)
1. **Settings → Accounts** — type your real current balance into the **start** box for
   Checking, Savings, and Cash. Add or rename accounts here too.
2. **Settings → Monthly Budgets** *(optional)* — set a spending limit per category.
   The dashboard bars turn amber near the limit and red when you go over.
3. **Paycheck → Direct Deposit Rules** — enter the fixed dollar amounts that always go
   to specific accounts (e.g. $500 → Savings), then pick your **remainder account**
   (gets whatever's left). Save.

## Daily use
- **Add** — type the amount, tap the account it came from, tap a category, save.
- **Calendar** — see your scheduled bills and paydays on a month grid. Tap any day for
  detail. Use **Today / ‹ ›** to move around.
- **Insights** — charts: income vs spending, category & account breakdowns, spending pace.

## Automate payday (every 2 weeks)
On the **Calendar** tab → **Schedule a Payday**: enter the amount, pick your next payday,
set **Repeats = Every 2 weeks**, and leave **Auto-post** on. From then on, each time you
open the app it deposits and splits that paycheck on its due date using your Paycheck
rules — no manual entry. Same idea for **Schedule a Bill** (rent, subscriptions, etc.).

> "Auto-post" runs when you open the app and catches anything due since you were last in.
> Anything due that *isn't* set to auto-post shows a gold banner up top with a "Post all"
> button.

## Daily budget & the savings advisor
Open **More → Budget Plan**. Enter your monthly take-home income and your fixed monthly
expenses (rent, utilities, subscriptions). You'll get three options:

- **Aggressive** — saves the most, leaves the smallest daily spend.
- **Base** — balanced.
- **Conservative** — saves the least, leaves the most to spend.

Each option shows how much you'd save per month, what you'd have saved in a year, and the
**daily amount you have to spend**. Tap **Use this plan** on the one you like.

After that, a green **"Available to spend today"** card floats at the top of **Home** (and
**Insights**). Every expense you log counts against it. If you don't spend the whole daily
amount, the leftover **rolls into tomorrow**; if you overspend, it comes out of the days
ahead. Tap **Adjust** anytime to change income, expenses, or your savings style.

## Your data
Everything saves automatically in this browser on this computer — no accounts, no cloud.
Use **Settings → Export backup** now and then to save a `.json` you can re-import or move
to another device. Clearing your browser data starts fresh, so keep a backup.
